We partnered with IEX Group to rethink their parent company website, as well their subsidiaries. Each site and brand was approached by first understanding their unique user and technology complexities to better communicate their value and then extending their individual brands to relate and gain trust with their intended end users. 

IEX Exchange

IEX Exchange, an innovative stock exchange facilitator built to serve investors, needed a website that could both serve their marketing needs and house a robust content and documentation library, all while also abiding by regulatory and security requirements.
