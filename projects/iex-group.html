IEX Group

Developed 2022-2023 at Whiteboard

  • Websites
  • Family of Brands
  • SASS

We partnered with IEX Group to reimagine the web presence of their parent brand and various subsidiaries. Each site was thoughtfully designed to address specific user needs, technical complexities, and regulatory requirements, while maintaining a consistent brand voice across the family. This approach helped build trust and clarity for diverse audiences from investors to developers, across multiple platforms. 

IEX Exchange

The flagship IEX Exchange website serves both as a marketing front and a resource hub. Designed to meet strict regulatory standards, it provides investors with clear, authoritative content alongside a comprehensive documentation library—all wrapped in a confident, accessible interface.

IEX swXtch

swXtch required a distinct site that communicates its unique technology and benefits to a sophisticated user base. We crafted a sleek, intuitive experience to highlight swXtch’s offerings and position it as a leader in the evolving fintech landscape.

↗ View the Site
IEX Cloud

IEX Cloud consolidates financial data for developers and businesses. We translated complex product information into a dynamic, user-friendly UI and website that clearly conveys its value proposition—making data access and integration straightforward and engaging.

IEX Group

We began to establish the parent brand and website for IEX to strategically align these sub-brands together. The client decided to take the endeavor in-house and these designs never moved forward.
