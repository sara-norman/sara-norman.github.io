Art is meant to be explored. The Hunter’s new website embodies that belief—transforming a static, information-heavy experience into an open invitation to wander, discover, and connect. Whether someone is planning a visit, exploring art from home, or diving into the museum’s stories, every detail is designed to guide them deeper into the world of the Hunter.

↗ View the Site
Breathing Life Into an Old Brand

The Hunter Museum is alive with color, conversation, and community—and now, so is its website. Creative direction centered on authenticity: warm tones inspired by the museum, photography that captures people engaging with art, and a design that feels approachable yet refined. The result is a digital space that reflects real experiences—kids sketching in galleries, friends talking over coffee, and moments of awe in front of a masterpiece.

End-Users in Mind

The site puts participation before transactions, making it easy to find what matters most in just two clicks. A dynamic events calendar, intuitive exhibition navigation, and smooth ticketing and membership flows keep the focus on art and community, not friction. Every interaction—whether buying tickets, joining as a member, or simply exploring—feels effortless, keeping visitors engaged before, during, and after their visit.
