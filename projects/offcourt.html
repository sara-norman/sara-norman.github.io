OffCourt

Completed 2024 at Whiteboard

  • Creative Direction
  • Digital Product / App
  • Go-to-market Strategy
  • Brand Positioning

OffCourt set out to build more than an app—they built a platform for athlete empowerment. A minimalist design paired with a robust feature set lets athletes take center stage. The result is a brand and digital experience that engages both athletes and fans, fostering connection and long-term loyalty.

Breathing Life Into a Timeless Concept

Rooted in Swiss modernism, OffCourt’s identity is clean, high-contrast, and timeless, anchored in Helvetica Neue with a mostly black-and-white palette. Strategic pops of color highlight moments of energy, while every touchpoint, from logos to onboarding flows, maintains clarity and consistency, letting each athlete’s personal brand shine.

User Acquisition and Experience

OffCourt needed to engage both athletes and fans. Acquisition-focused landing pages, refined onboarding flows, and light and dark modes made the experience personal and accessible. Athletes get a streamlined Insights dashboard for performance and engagement, while fans enjoy an elevated feed, shop, and interactive player profiles. Every update, from component reskins to shareable moments, makes connecting, exploring, and engaging effortless.
