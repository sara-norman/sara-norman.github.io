Arclin

Completed 2024 at Whiteboard

  • Brand Extension
  • Accessibility
  • Creative Direction
  • Website

Arclins' new site was the definition of a team effort. Together we pushed a print-forward brand into an immersive experience, organized their vast offerings and products to a clear user experience, and demanded accessibility and ease of use from all possible entry points.

↗ View the Site
From UnderConsideration's Brand New review

“As conservative as the rationalization was for the logo, the website is kind of bonkers, in a good way, adopting a relatively in-your-face attitude with big type, punchy graphics, and lots of unexpected UI details. Overall, a great corporate identity that avoids the dryness of the category but also doesn’t succumb to making it all friendly and cute and instead doubles down on its scientific rigor and innovation.”

End-Users in Mind

We created a structured system that made finding products effortless, organizing everything by industry, category, and brand so users could quickly find what they needed. Throughout the site, we put people at the center of the experience—not just products. The visual design balances technical precision with warmth, ensuring visitors feel engaged rather than overwhelmed. Navigation is clean, pages flow naturally, and the entire experience is intuitive, whether someone is searching for a product or learning about Arclin’s industry impact.
