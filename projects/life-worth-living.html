Life Worth Living

Completed 2024 at Whiteboard

  • Branding
  • Asset Creation
  • Content Library

In collaboration with the Yale Center for Faith and Culture, Life Worth Living was developed as a content-rich platform to inspire thoughtful engagement with culture and spirituality. The platform combines a serene, inviting brand with a web experience designed to encourage curiosity, discovery, and optimism.

↗ View the Site
Breathing Life Into a New Philosophy

Rooted in editorial clarity, Life Worth Living’s branding emphasizes simplicity and empowerment. The logo draws from classic wordmarks and monograms, while a carefully curated color palette and open-license imagery system provides Yale’s team with the tools to confidently create, organize, and categorize content across the platform.

End-Users in Mind

The browsing experience is designed like a curated rabbit hole, inviting users to explore, question, and learn at their own pace. Every interaction fosters a sense of calm and optimism, guiding visitors through complex cultural and spiritual themes with clarity and purpose.
