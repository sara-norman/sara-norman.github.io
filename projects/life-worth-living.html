Life Worth Living

Completed 2024 at Whiteboard

  • Branding
  • Asset Creation
  • Content Library

Working with Yale Center for Faith and Culture, we helped their team dream up the next chapter of their course program, Life Worth Living. LWL inspires readers to engage in the nuances of culture, religion, and spirituality in order to connect with themselves, others, and the world around us. We formed a brand based around serenity and curiosity and extended this to their content-heavy web presence. 

↗ View the Site
Brand

Life Worth Living’s brand was influenced by editorial design, thus we wanted the logo to fall in line with the clear wordmarks/monogram of the industry. We also wanted Yale’s team to feel empowered creating their content assets, so we proposed colors, open license imagery, and a color system that aligned with their taxonomy.

Website

Our browsing experience intended to feel like a curated rabbit trail... encouraging the user to explore, question, read, and learn for themselves. The user should be left with peace and optimism for the world we live in.
