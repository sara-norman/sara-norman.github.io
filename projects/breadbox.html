Breadbox

Completed 2024 at Whiteboard

  • Branding
  • Asset Creation
  • Web App Design

Junior Achievement’s mission to empower youth with economic skills aligns perfectly with Chick-fil-A’s vision through Breadbox, a scalable, game-inspired learning platform. The platform delivers expert content in a playful, motivating way that encourages learners to build skills, track progress, and explore financial concepts without pressure or stigma.

↗ View the Site
Breathing Life Into a Trusted Brand

Reflecting Breadbox’s optimistic and accessible approach, the brand embraces bright colors, playful iconography, and a flexible system of assets, from gamified badges to course graphics. This evolving visual language creates a cohesive, encouraging experience across every touchpoint.

Learning Made Engaging

Built on WordPress with LifterLMS and custom features, the Breadbox web app combines expert-written content with intentional gamification and personalized progress tracking. Every interaction is designed to spark curiosity, remove barriers, and make financial education approachable, relevant, and rewarding.
