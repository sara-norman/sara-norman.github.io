Land ID

Completed 2023 at Whiteboard

  • Brand Extention
  • Website

In 2023, Land ID merged companies and needed a fresh visual presence to reflect their evolution. Partnering closely with their in-house Creative Director, the goal was to translate their newly developed brand into a flexible, scalable system—one that could clearly highlight their SaaS product and resonate with diverse users.

↗ View the Site
Brand Evolution

Land ID’s in-house team had already established a beautiful foundation. Our role was to interpret and expand it into a living, functional system. By layering product components with real-life visuals, we balanced the sophistication of a SaaS platform with clear, relatable communication. In the end, ensuring the product remained approachable and user-focused.

Website

OThe new website was designed to make it easy for users to explore features, interact with examples, and understand the product’s value at a glance. Their in-house team took the site back before launch, ensuring full ownership as the brand continues to evolve.
